About Jorge Amezcua

and

Steadfast Motifs

My work analyzes visual communications which contribute to the advancement and even degeneration of our place in the world; a world bound by practicality and power. My studies in semiotics and communication design fuel my purpose to contribute responsibly to the advancement of my people. Visual communications is a tool that I wield in the challenge to share abstract concepts and ideas.

Steadfast Motifs comes from a desire to push brands forward in their quest to promote new innovative products and services. My goal is to help clients reach new audiences through responsible representation. In short, our clients can trust that their ideas and beliefs, as well as outcomes, will be given the upmost respect.
