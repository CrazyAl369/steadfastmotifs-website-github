Marina Highschool Mural

Working on the Marina High School Mural was one of my favorite experiences at CSUMB. I was enrolled in a large scale painting and mural course and part of the classwork consisted of rendering a mural at the Marina High School.

Judy Baca (on the left) blessed us with good vibes today. She is an artist, activist, and Professor of Chicano Studies and World Arts & Cultures.

We met just a couple days a week but the project came together very quickly. You can see me painting a rounded brain coral on the bottom right of the second image.

Collaborating is beautiful, especially under Johanna Poethig's instruction. Non-woven medium application was practiced on this project. This process allows us to attach a painted image onto most surfaces.

Passing period just got a lot more colorful. This mural will really bring the students together. No more staring at blank walls, now there is a sea of color to captivate the minds and hearts of the young scholars attend the Marina High School.
