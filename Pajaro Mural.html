Pajaro Park Mural

This wall, measuring at a solid 300 feet, is a great example of creatives coming together collaboratively to create space and beautify the community. The Arts Council for Monterey County has done marvelously in their mission to bring arts to the school systems in our area.

Artistic director/ professor Johanna Poethig, with the help of alumni and students of the visual public art department at CSUMB, were able to design public works at large scale. Special thanks to Gail and also our friends from the Art Council.

Professional development was definitely happening in every level of education, it was also a great treat to work with Jose Ortiz and his students on this new recreational facility located in the heart of a largely farmworker community.

The artworks of children in Pajaro schools, working with Kenji were used as references.
